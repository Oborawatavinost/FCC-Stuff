Kimura Kanon (木村カノン)

A picture of KANON.
KANON is a member of Atarashii Gakkou no Leaders (新しい学校のリーダーズ; lit.: New School Leaders) is a Japanese girl group under ASOBISYSTEM. Their lineup consists of MIZYU, RIN, SUZUKA, and KANON. They made their Japanese debut on June 7, 2017, with the single ‘Dokubana.’ They promote internationally under the name ATARASHII GAKKO!.
